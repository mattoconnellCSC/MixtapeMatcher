User Stories
1. As a person with a very narrow taste in music, I want to look at my friends' digital mixtapes, so that I can explore more genres of music. 
2. As a person who likes a wide variety of music, I want to let my friends guess which digital mixtapes I made, so that I can see if they will recognize my unique personality based on my taste.
3. As a person who loves all sorts of music, I want to have my friends guess which digital mixtapes are mine, so that their incorrect guesses will let me know which of my friends have similar taste to me.
