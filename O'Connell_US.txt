User Stories


As a user, I want to meet and keep track of new friends that play too, so that I can have more fun with more people.


As a user, I want to guess which of my friends made a playlist, so that I can test how well I know them, and have fun with them.


As a user, I want to listen to music through the application, so that I can more easily browse my friends’ selections.
